Why Lithium-Air?

Lithium-air batteries are seen as one of the most promising candidates for ultra-high energy storage due to their theoretical energy density being up to 10x higher than conventional lithium-ion batteries. This makes them especially attractive for:

  • Electric vehicles (EVs) with extended range
  • Grid-scale storage for renewable energy
  • Portable electronics with longer battery life
